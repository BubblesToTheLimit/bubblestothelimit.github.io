healing

These “bits” I share here are short pieces of information that came up while being in meditative states. While in strictly logical terms they might not make sense to you, for fellow meditators the probably mean alot. Either way, you might still find some of them to be inspiring.

At a certain height of experience, let the feeling of openness and enough-ness echo and vibrate through your being, especially the stomach. Meditate on it. Unconscious processes diminish the stomach power and can become a default. Make a new decision. #healing

What is closed can be opened. 🧘‍♂️

Only what we’re aware of can be healed and transformed. Everything after gaining the awareness is easy and straightforward. For that of course first we have to look into the right direction… #healing

Stress is the process of suppressing something internally in order to survive. Therefor it should always be a conscious decision, not an unconscious default we keep living in. #healing

Our society is too “brainy”. 🧘‍♂️

Explanation: Brainy in quotes since if course real brainyness / intelligence would be to be healthy at all levels. Our society deals with many self inflicted diseases, i.e stress. Its not very smart to make yourself sick, is it? #healing

The belly is the authority for health and power. Staying in the head definitely has to cause stress, since the brain doesnt have that authority and knowing over health. Being able and allowing yourself to move between brain, heart & stomach “thinking” is healthy. #healing
