meditation

These “bits” I share here are short pieces of information that came up while being in meditative states. While in strictly logical terms they might not make sense to you, for fellow meditators the probably mean alot. Either way, you might still find some of them to be inspiring.

Dont sleep in #meditation. Since you’re evolving into a more powerful being, you must be shaking with intensity.

The question of earth is this: When you’re confused and feeling weak, will you still recognize and manifest the truth which cannot be denied? #spirituality #meditation

Dont give your own genious such easy tasks all the time. Let it fly, let it rip - let it do its playful dance (in #meditation). #spirituality

When they asked the #yogi what he was doing, he answered: everything. #spirituality #meditation

Life is a gift if we’re willing to unpack it and look inside. #meditation #spirituality #theorizing

Alan Watts once famously said “the godhead cannot see itself”. Yes, true, but nevertheless: The godhead can be itself. When you connect with your higher self in #meditation this becomes super important. The active god wouldnt need to look at himself in the mirror. #spirituality

#Meditation is like building a castle out of a slightly shittier version of that same castle on that same spot. And it all starts with wondering if you should pick up that shovel. #theorizing

Only when we forget about work completely during the weekend, can we start fresh on monday. #theorizing #meditation

As long as you feel bad there is always this urge to solve problems, to get busy and to chase this and that goal with no end in sight. As soon as you feel good, well, there isn’t. #meditation #calmingdown #lostinthought

Start listening to your state of being and you can stop watching the movie of your projection #meditation #lostinthought
